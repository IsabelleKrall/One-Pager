CUSTOMIZE YOUR OWN BIKE

Prove to the world that there’s no such thing as clashing colours. For designers and those with artistic sensibilities, our custom bikes give you complete autonomy to literally modify the nuts off our bikes. Unleash your creativity and make a one-off fixie, single speed or 16 speed bike that you actually want to ride from millions of different combinations.

BUILD YOUR OG

Never ride stock. design your own OG road bike, to suit your style. If none of our set designs take your fancy, don’t worry, because you can build your dream bike from the ground up, choosing from 8 to 9 colours per component, the possibilities are endless. Once you’re done deciding on the finishing touches, hit add to cart and our team of bike builders will build your dream bike to spec and ship directly to your door.

LIFE'S COMPLICATED ENOUGH

We’ve been bent on making the best single speed bike on the market, and we’ve finally nailed it. This is it; the alpha, omega, first, and last. The only bike you’ll ever need for a simple ride and a simpler life. Globally loved and praised, it’s now even better value than before.

OUR STORY

Like any story worth telling, our journey from bikes to boardroom has more than one chapter. In 2017 Mango hit a bump, like those potholes that send you head-over-handlebars. It was a mix of good bikes, bad numbers, and some not-so-wise prophets. Things went a little sour, like those mangos that just don’t taste right, so it was time for a change. Instead of lounging around in defeat, we did what riders do. We got Mango back on the bike – metaphorically first, but then for real. We relocated to Ballyclare in Northern Ireland, where we still make the same brilliant bikes, but with twice the rainfall and a nicer view.
